Opera News

Yulia Fayn opera director from Israel, laureate of the Ist International Competition of Young Opera Directors “Nano Opera” She won the Main Award of the Media Jury. Yulia Fayn graduated the Russian Academy of Theatre Arts the workshop of Professors Georgy Ansimov and Dmitry Bertman as stage director and Russian State Academy by Gnessin as a Choir conductor. She have been worked as an assistant of famous Russian director Gregory Ansimov for some works, include the World Premiere of D. Talankin "Citizen Tsvetaeva" and musical "Don Quixote” during two years. In 2013 year Yullia Fayn was invited as Programme Director for staging Russian on - line TV show “Grand Opera“ on the National Russian TV Channel “Kultura“ Her working with young opera singers and yours bright ideas were appreciated very highly both tv-- audition and operas critics.

WOLFGANG AMADEUS MOZART “ SHADOW OF DON GIOVANNI”

Opera Theatre RGSAI
19, 21, 23, 25 May 2022
The performance "Shadows of Don Juan" was born from Mozart's opera scenes. The director Yulia Fayn worked on them and observed how similar some of Mozart's characters are to each other, how one person develops, changes and goes from composition to composition. The performances show to trace these transformations and created a performance about them.
Music director and conductor - Freddy Cadena

Dmitry Bertman General and artistic director of the «Helikon-opera» theatre, Moscow  Letter of recommendation

Yulia Fayn studied in my workshop at Russian Academy of Theater Arts (Moscow) and graduated in 2011. She successfully defended her graduation performances: the operas of Debussy "L’enfant prodigue" and Pergolesi "Il Maestro di musica". During her studies and while working on performances, Yulia showed herself as a talented, versatile director, an excellent organizer and a responsible person. Yulia carefully and attentively works with artists, she has excellent musical training and a fine sense of music. I can recommend her as a gifted and responsible professional.
